Key Insight
The real shift isn't
writing code faster.
It's thinking differently.
AI doesn't just autocomplete your lines. It changes what you decide to build. When the cost of implementation drops to near zero, the bottleneck shifts from "can we build it" to "should we build it."

The best developers in 2025 aren't the fastest typists. They're the ones who ask the right questions and design the right abstractions.
